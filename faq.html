How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

  1. Make sure you can log in with your HZ account (you access HZ webapps through the MyHZ dashboard → “Apps”).
  2. Add money to your HZ print account if needed (this is the official HZ top-up portal).
  3. Install/choose the HZ “Follow-Me” print queue from the MyHZ/Apps area (the exact installer/guide is behind login in the Apps portal).
  4. Send your document to the HZ Follow-Me queue from your laptop.
  5. Go to any campus multifunction printer, sign in with your HZ card, and release the job (your HZ card is the one used for printing/copying on campus).

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

  1. Tap your HZ card to log in at any multifunction printer (same card used for printing/copying)
  2. Choose Scan → Scan to Email (select your HZ email) → set options (PDF, duplex, resolution) → Start. The file will arrive in your HZ inbox, ready to download to your laptop.

How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?

  1. Go to MyHZ → Apps → Webshop (HZ’s internal web shop is at ; it requires HZ login).
  2. Browse the categories, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout.
  3. Complete the payment as offered in the shop for that item (method varies per product in the internal shop).
  4. You will receive the order confirmation by email and/or see it under your Webshop account.

How can you book a project space?

    Community/Project spaces on campus

  1. Use the HZ Community Hub in Vlissingen when an informal collaborative area works (large project table, benches, small theatre). If you need a specific room/time, ask locally at the Service Desk for the booking procedure for that building.

    2. Through MyHZ / Service Desk

  2. Many room/services requests are handled via the internal Apps/Service portals (behind login). If you do not see a “Room/Space reservation” app in MyHZ, contact the Service Desk for your location; they will either book it for you or point you to the correct internal form.
